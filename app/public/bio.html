Hello, Wine Enthusiasts!

My name is Lindsey Giddens and I am an Executive Consultant with Scout & Cellar - a clean crafted wine company founded in August 2017. My passion for what I do is inspired by our fearless leader, Sarah Shadonix, and her goal to find cleaner, healthier, better wine options.


What does that mean?


By partnering with organic vineyards, Scout & Cellar has started an initiative to remove unnecessary additives from wine. We only only sell clean, biodynamic wines which eliminate hangovers, headaches, and other adverse side effects that tend to accompany mass produced wine. I joined Scout & Cellar due to my love of all things wine and my distaste for hangovers.
